About Me.

Hi, I'm Cille Kirchner, I'm a 3rd year Graphic and Digital Media student at Artesis Plantijn University. During my education I've developed a true passion for video editing and everything that goes with it. I studied abroad for a semester in the UK at Notthingham Trent University. Here I followed the course Filmmaking.

My Knowledge.

I'm addicted to learning new things and broadening my knowledge, I love working with After Effects and Premiere Pro. I recently learned how to work with Davinci Resolve. I instantly liked working with this software and I've been doing a lot of self studying to further improve my skills. Even though video editing is my main interest, those aren't the only programs that I've come into contact with during my education. Some examples include: Illustrator, Indesign and Photoshop.

My Interests.

I love reading, I'm learning how to play the guitar and every summer I go hiking in the Alps. I listen to all kinds of music but my favourite genres are Metal and Drum and Bass. Since I like to stay fit, I also go to the gym.
