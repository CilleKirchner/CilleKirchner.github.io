About Me.

Hi, I'm Cille Kirchner, I'm a 3rd year Graphic and Digital Media student at Artesis Plantijn University. Currently I'm studying abroad in the UK at Nottingham Trent University, here I'm following the course Filmmaking.

My Interests.

I'm a 22 year old student who has a passion for video editing. However that's not all I like. When I have free time I like to read, I'm trying to learn how to play the guitar. I listen to all kinds of music but my favourite genres are Metal and Drum and Bass. Since I like to stay fit, I also go to the gym.

I love learning new things and broaden my knowledge, I love working with After Effects and Premiere Pro. Howerver, I recently learned hwo to work with Davinci Resolve, and I've been doing a lot of self studying. I like the workflow of it better than Premiere Pro, but I don't know everything there's to know about it yet.
